# Flowchestra Product Context

## Flowchestra Blurb (for system prompts)

Flowchestra is a secure AI operations platform designed to help organizations safely adopt and operationalize AI in their day-to-day work. It provides a vendor-neutral environment where users can interact with AI through chat, agents, and automated workflows while enforcing governance, security, and observability by default.

Data and activity within Flowchestra are organized into Workspaces and Workbenches. Workspaces represent organizational boundaries such as business units, departments, or teams, while Workbenches represent projects, initiatives, or focused areas of work within those Workspaces. This structure allows organizations to maintain clear separation of context, permissions, and memory while enabling collaboration where appropriate.

External systems integrate with Flowchestra through Tools and Toolkits. Tools represent specific capabilities or actions (such as querying a database, sending an email, or retrieving documents), while Toolkits group related tools together around a service or integration. These integrations allow AI agents and users to interact with business systems in a controlled, policy-enforced manner.

With capabilities like real-time data sanitization, agent guardrails, policy enforcement, and AI observability, Flowchestra ensures sensitive information is protected while AI activity remains auditable and governed. The result is a platform where organizations can confidently deploy AI across their operations without sacrificing security, compliance, or control.
